Blog Playbook

The FXIFY standard. Every article ships with this. No exceptions. 📋

✦ Required in every article

Summary table

For articles over 800 words. 4 columns, 4-5 rows of key facts at the top.

Table of contents

For articles over 800 words. Anchor links to each H2 section.

FAQ section

Minimum 5 questions. Direct, concise answers. Major SEO opportunity.

Key Takeaways block

5-7 bullets at the end. Readers who skim get the full value.

Internal links

Minimum 2-3 links to relevant FXIFY pages. Don't leave link equity on the table.

Author byline

Every article gets a real author name. No ghost writing. Select your name in the brief.

◈ Keywords & SEO

Primary keyword in H1 and at least 2 H2 headings

Secondary keywords in H2s and body — naturally, never stuffed

Bold 8-10 key terms and stats throughout the article

Every H2 must have at least one bolded phrase or statistic

≡ Scannability rules

Max 20 words per sentence

Max 3 sentences per paragraph

Use bullet points and numbered lists — not walls of text

Break long sections with H3 subheadings

⚡ FXIFY voice

Confident. We know trading. No hedging or over-qualifying.

Direct. Short sentences. Get to the point fast.

Second person. Always "you" — talk to the trader directly.

Accurate on drawdown. When in doubt, check fxify.com/rules before writing.

✗ Never do this

Start with "In this article we will..."

Vague language — "some traders may find..." or "it is possible that..."

Long paragraphs with no bolding or structure

Skip the FAQ or internal links to save time

Make claims about FXIFY that are not on fxify.com

Submit without checking keyword placement in headings

Reviewing

✍️ Your job on this draft

1

Fact-check everything

Verify all stats, rules and claims against fxify.com. Flag or fix anything that looks invented. Drawdown rules especially.

2

Humanise the intro & conclusion

Rewrite the opening paragraph in your own voice. Add a real trading observation or hook. The AI draft is a skeleton — make it sound like a person.

3

Add experience signals

Insert at least one "At FXIFY, we've seen..." or a real trader scenario. This is what makes us credible to Google and readers.

4

Bold key points & stats

Go through the whole article and bold important phrases, statistics and key terms. Aim for 8-10 bolded items minimum.

5

Replace internal link placeholders

Find every [INTERNAL LINK] placeholder and replace with a real FXIFY URL. Use the links panel on the left. Minimum 2-3 per article.

6

Upload images

Click each image placeholder and upload a relevant visual. Don't submit with empty placeholders.

7

Read it aloud

If it sounds robotic, fix it. Short sentences. Cut anything that doesn't add value. Max 20 words per sentence.

8

Tick the checklist before submitting

Every item on the left sidebar checklist should be ticked. If it isn't, the article isn't ready.
